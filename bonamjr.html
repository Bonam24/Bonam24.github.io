    Community engagement

    I have done several projects which have benefitted my community. My community projects consists of projects which have received external funding and projects which have not received funding and have been championed by me.

    Nkamlikum maths project

    The world is in the fourth industrial revolution and my community lack personnel with the required digital skills to help the community reap the benefits which come with the fourth industrial revolution. This is because a lot students shy away from STEM majors. Less than 2 students in every 50 students in my community consider studying computer science in high school because they do not understand mathematics. I have intervened in this by providing students with toll free classes so that they can be able to gain interest in mathematics and computer science

    Mbu Bakundu Project

    Mbu Bakundu village is a remote commmunity which has not got a road since its existence Almost every child in that community is ignorant of ICT. They do not even know that computers exists. This has been a challenge for several years. In order to solve this i championed a project which solicited for funds from the Mastercard foundation to install a solar plate in the village. After this installation was done, a computer and a tv were installed where children in that community so that students can be exposed to basic ICT.
